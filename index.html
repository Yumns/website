Coin Collecting

I am an avid coin collector. I enjoy collecting silver coins as they are guaranteed to appreciate over time (unless we find large quantities of silver in space and make mining it economically feasible). I also like collecting them because they are shiny.
I also collect coins from India. In the past 60 years, Indian coins have changed greatly from large copper coins to small steel ones. The most interesting Indian coins I have are fractional coins called paise (पैसे, 100 naye paise in 1 rupee). I have 2, 3, 5, 10, 20, 25, and 50 paise coins, my favorites among those being 2 and 3 owing to their unique shapes.
